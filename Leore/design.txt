~~~ The Orbs ~~~

- Star Orb (DONE):
	- shoots stars in linear direction
	- Stars destroy blocks

- Key Orb (DONE):
	- Grabs keys
	
- Crimson Orb (DONE):

- Fire Orb (DONE):

- Void Orb (DONE):

- Lightning Orb:

- Earth Orb:

- Ice Orb (DONE):
